BOOK DIANI TOUR Escape to Chale Island, Kenya’s private island paradise, just 10km south of Diani Beach. This full-day tour offers a scenic boat ride across turquoise waters, with opportunities for snorkeling, swimming, and birdwatching amidst vibrant coral reefs and mangrove forests. Explore the Gazi Women’s Boardwalk, relax on pristine white-sand beaches, and enjoy a delicious seafood lunch at The Sands at Chale Island Resort & Spa. Perfect for couples, families, or adventure seekers, this tour blends relaxation and exploration in a breathtaking tropical setting. Pricing Prices start at approximately $80-$120 per person, including hotel transfers, entrance fees, guided tours, and lunch. Costs vary based on group size, season, and operator. BOOK MOMBASA TOUR Immerse yourself in Mombasa, Kenya’s coastal gem blending Swahili, Arab, and Portuguese heritage. This full-day tour visits the historic Fort Jesus Museum, the vibrant Old Town with its narrow streets and spice markets, and the iconic Elephant Tusks on Moi Avenue. Explore the Akamba Wood Carving Center and enjoy lunch at a seaside restaurant like Tamarind Mombasa. Ideal for history buffs and culture seekers, this tour captures Mombasa’s multicultural charm and bustling port city vibe. Price Overview Prices start at $80-$120 per person, covering hotel transfers, entrance fees, guided tours, and lunch. Prices depend on group size, season, and operator. Reach out for exclusive deals and group discounts.

BOOK KISUMU TOUR Discover Kisumu, Kenya’s vibrant lakeside city on Lake Victoria, known for its rich Luo culture and scenic beauty. This half-day tour explores key attractions like the bustling Kibuye Market, the Kisumu Museum showcasing Nyanza Province artifacts, and the serene Dunga Beach for stunning lake views and fresh tilapia. Visit Hippo Point for iconic sunsets and the Kisumu Impala Sanctuary to see wildlife like impalas and zebras. Perfect for culture enthusiasts and nature lovers, this tour offers a glimpse into Kisumu’s history, trade heritage, and relaxed charm. Price Overview Prices range from $50-$80 per person, including hotel transfers, guided tours, and entrance fees. Costs may vary based on group size and season.
